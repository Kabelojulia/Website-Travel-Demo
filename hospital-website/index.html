about us

What is SurvyMash

SURVYMASH Pty (Ltd) is a 100% black owned company that provides quality transportation solutions within the borders of South Africa. The company was founded in 2017 by Tlhookomelo Survival Mashile.
The company started out as a basic transportation service provider which outsourced its commercial and private vehicles, it has since grown exponentially and now has its own fleet of commercial and private vehicles. SURVYMASH is fast becoming one of the most highly rated and professional private shuttling service provider in South Africa.
